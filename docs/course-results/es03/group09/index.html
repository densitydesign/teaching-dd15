Can you break up with a dating app?

Click on a circle to watch a narrative video portraying the experience of one of five people going through the account deletion process in different dating apps.

Badoo

It takes 6 steps to delete a Badoo account.

  1. Tap My Account at the bottom right of the screen.
  2. Scroll down and tap Account, where the email address associated to the account is.
  3. Tap Delete account in small grey text at the bottom of the screen.
  4. Pick Delete your account and tap Continue.
  5. Tap No, delete my account in small grey text at the bottom of the screen.
  6. Pick a reason and press the Delete account button.

Grindr

It takes 6 steps to delete a Grindr account.

  1. Tap Your profile at the top left of the screen.
  2. Tap Settings.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete profile in grey.
  4. Pick a reason or tap Skip.
  5. Write the email address associated to the account and tap Delete Profile.
  6. Tap Delete My Account and All Data.

Happn

It takes 5 steps to delete a Happn account.

  1. Tap My Account at the bottom right of the screen.
  2. Scroll down to Legal Notices and tap My data.
  3. Tap Delete my account.
  4. Tap Delete my account in grey at the bottom of the screen.
  5. Pick a reason and tap Yes, I’m done!

Mamba

It takes 7 steps to delete a Mamba account.

  1. Tap My Account at the bottom right of the screen.
  2. Tap Settings at the top right of the screen.
  3. Tap General.
  4. Tap Delete your profile at the bottom left of the screen.
  5. Tap Delete your profile.
  6. In the confirmation email sent by Mamba, click Delete.
  7. Over on mamba.ru, follow the instructions on screen to complete the deletion.

Zoosk

It takes 7 steps to delete a Zoosk account, but unfortunately it’s not permanent.

  1. Go to zoosk.com on a web browser and log into your account.
  2. Click on Account at the top right of the screen.
  3. Select Account settings.
  4. Click on Account Status and choose Deactivate Account.
  5. Click on Deactivation.
  6. Pick one item on the list and click on Continue Deactivation.
  7. Confirm Deactivation once again.

About the project

We have taken a look at five popular dating apps:

  • Badoo
  • Grindr
  • Happn
  • Mamba
  • Zoosk

We determined how many steps it takes to permanently delete account on each one. To make it easier to understand, we crafted five narrative videos to show the account deletion process and demonstrate how frustrating it can be, as well as providing step-by-step guides.

Dating apps make account deletion purposely difficult or at times impossible, leading to very personal information of yours staying online against your wishes.

Online dating platforms enable individuals to find and connect with new people over the internet, with the intent to develop personal, romantic or sexual relationships. The vast majority of online dating services can be accessed from personal computers and mobile devices alike.

In order to work, dating apps request a lot of information from potential new members: pictures, physical locations, habits, health information and much more. This information is valuable, in that it is used to matchmake dates through the platforms’ enigmatic algorithms.

For more information on what dating apps ask of their users, check out What kind of information is required by 10 different dating applications and which are unique among them? from Digital dating: faking oneself & stolen profiles.
