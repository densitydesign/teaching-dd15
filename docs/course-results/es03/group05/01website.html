    “Law of the Jungle” shows the controversial side of online censorship on Instagram through the less visible elements that regulate it, the policies. The project was born during the last phase of the Final Synthesis Design Studio at the Politecnico di Milano. The course, held by the Density Design research department, aims to explore different social issues through the analysis of data contents.
